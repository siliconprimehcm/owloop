Why do criminals and criminal organizations traffic in humans? This question can be answered with one word: money. The United Nations estimates the total number of people trafficked every year is between 700 thousand and 1 million. It also estimates that 79 percent of these victims are trafficked for sexual exploitation. Human trafficking takes place in every country and it generates tens of billions of dollars of profit each year.
